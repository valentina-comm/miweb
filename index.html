LION TAMER PROFESSION

Would you like to be a lion tamer?

That sounds like an exciting and challenging job! A lion tamer is someone who trains and handles these majestic animals in a controlled environment, such as a circus, zoo, or show.

Some of the responsibilities of a lion tamer might include:

* Feeding and caring for the lions
* Training the lions to perform specific behaviors
* Interacting with the lions safely and respectfully
* Educating the public about lion biology and behavior
